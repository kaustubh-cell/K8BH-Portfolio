# K8BH Portfolio 

Hey! This is my personal website, built for Week 1 of Hack Club's Resolution pathway. 

I wanted something clean, modern, and easy to read without relying on templates. It's basically my digital business card where I can drop my projects as I learn and build more things.

## What's on the site?
- **Dark & Light Mode:** Because staring at a white screen at 2 AM hurts. You can toggle it at the top right.
- **Typewriter Effect:** A cool little text animation in the main header.
- **The Vibe Check:** A button that tracks your clicks and fills up a progress bar (just to prove my JavaScript actually works).
- **Project Grid:** A dedicated spot to link the stuff I build next.

## What I used to build it
- **HTML5:** For the skeleton and structure.
- **CSS3:** For the layout, colors, grid, and animations.
- **Vanilla JavaScript:** For the interactive stuff (the theme toggle, typing effect, and counter).
- Absolutely zero bloated frameworks. 

## How to run it
You don't need any crazy setup, terminals, or servers to view this site locally. 

1. Download or clone this folder.
2. Double-click `index.html`.
3. It will open right up in your web browser. That's literally it.
